Escape

I designed a corporate identity guideline booklet for a fictitious food corporation called Escape. Escape is an all natural company that produces products such as; therapeutic teas, fluoride free tooth paste, green tea tincture, and green tea mints. My goal was to create a well-trusted product containing of the purest of ingredients containing of no toxic chemicals and no artificial coloring or flavors. Escape commits itself to the highest ethical and professional conduct for its employees and the environment.

Experience Recreation

I worked with the design team to create a new logo for University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire’s Recreation department. This department went away from a independent logo to something that was unified under the University brand.

Annual Report

This was a 18 page Annual Report that was created and sent off to print in 6 hours. Sometimes things don't always go as planned and for this project I was able to use my strength of working well under pressure to get the job done.

Fusion Cafe

The Fusion Cafe is a fictitious coffee and sandwich shop with an extra bonus of an evironment that helps support artists by offering various live entertainment every weekend. My design goal was to create something clean, fresh and user freindly.

Leadership Seminar Series

My student career has allowed the opportunity to work on the collaborative project with my co-worker Josh Thorson. The Leadership Seminar Series is a casual and fun program designed to provide students with an opportunity to explore leadership with a small group of peers and apply what is learned to real life experiences. Our design goal was to help encourage students to participate in these seminars.
