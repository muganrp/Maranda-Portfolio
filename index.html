Who am I, you ask?

I'm a friendly midwest gal that studied Graphic Design and Business Administration to strengthen my marketing and design skills. My objective is to utilize my creative soul and challenge myself to think critically to solve problems. I am a hard working individual and an excellent team player. I have over four years experience in marketing and design field and aim to never stop growing.

Nonprofit Organization Logo

This logo is for a friend of mine that is starting a nonprofit organization for kids in Foster Care. Her inspiration for Lifted UP was stemmed from her own personal experiences with foster care. Her plan is to provide kids with the essentials, such as a new duffle bag, blanket, pillow, clothes, toiletries, etc. in hopes to make their transition much more accommodating.
